About Us

Work Experience and Business Record

The founder, Dipl.Ing.(TU) Dipl.Kfm.(FH) Frank D. Wieland has multiple years of industry experience in leadership roles in domestic and cross-border projects, ranging from Industrial and Commercial construction, Hospitals to Office and High-Rise building construction. He and his team of experts are used to handle complex, eight digits worth projects. With his voluntary add-on education as certified Business Mediator and his many years of experience in leadership roles he is your perfect partner for your construction-related projects! 

Business Network

Wieland Baumanagement Consult is part of an extensive network of construction engineering offices throughout Germany comprising over 25 highly-skilled engineers. As part of this network we are able to assist you and your projects even on a short notice. We are especially focused on rescuing construction sites about to fail.
